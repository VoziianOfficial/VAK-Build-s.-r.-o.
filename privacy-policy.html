VAK-Build-s.-r.-o. respects your privacy. This document describes the types of information we may collect through this website, why we use it, and how you can contact us about privacy-related questions.

Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the website operated for VAK-Build-s.-r.-o. When you browse the website, submit a contact form, accept or decline cookies, or communicate with us by email, certain information may be processed so we can operate the website and respond to your request.

This website is intended to present digital marketing, advertising, SEO, web design, conversion, and local visibility services. It is not intended to collect sensitive personal data.

Data categories

Information we collect

We may collect information that you provide directly and limited technical information generated when you use the website.

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, selected service, and message.
  • Communication information if you contact us by email.
  • Basic technical data such as browser type, device type, pages viewed, and interaction signals.
  • Cookie preference data, such as whether you accepted or declined the cookie banner.
No unnecessary sensitive data.

Please do not submit confidential, financial, health, or other sensitive information through the contact form.

Purpose

How we use information

We use information only for practical website and communication purposes connected to the services presented on this website.

  • To respond to contact requests and service inquiries.
  • To understand which service you are interested in.
  • To maintain website functionality and basic security.
  • To improve website clarity, navigation, and user experience.
  • To respect your cookie preference.
Cookies

Cookies and similar technologies

This website may use cookies or local storage to remember your cookie banner choice and support basic website functionality. Optional analytics or marketing cookies should only be used according to your consent and the cookie settings implemented on the website.

You can read more in our Cookie Policy. You can also adjust cookie settings in your browser.

Disclosure

How information may be shared

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary for website operation, communication, service administration, or legal compliance.

  • With website hosting or technical service providers.
  • With email or communication tools used to respond to your request.
  • With analytics or security tools where applicable and permitted.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legitimate legal request.
Retention

How long we keep information

We keep information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, such as responding to inquiries, maintaining business records, improving the website, or meeting legal obligations.

Contact form submissions and email messages may be retained for a reasonable period so we can manage communication history and follow up on service requests.

Your control

Your privacy rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights related to your personal information. These may include the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or a copy of certain information.

To make a privacy request, contact us at support@vakbuildmedia.com. We may need to verify your request before taking action.

Protection

Security

We use reasonable organizational and technical measures to protect information submitted through the website. However, no website, email system, or internet transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Please use caution when sending information online and avoid submitting sensitive or confidential information through website forms.
