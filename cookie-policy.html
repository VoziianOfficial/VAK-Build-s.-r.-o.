Cookies and similar technologies help websites remember information, support basic functionality, and understand how visitors interact with pages. This policy explains the types of technologies that may be used on this website.

Cookie Policy

Introduction

This Cookie Policy applies to the website operated for VAK-Build-s.-r.-o. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

When you visit this website, a cookie banner may ask you to accept or decline cookies. Your choice may be stored so the banner does not appear repeatedly.

Definition

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website or browser. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, pixels, scripts, and tags that help websites remember settings or measure basic interactions.

  • Session cookies may be removed when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies may remain until they expire or are deleted.
  • Local storage may keep simple preference data in your browser.
  • Third-party tools may set their own cookies depending on implementation and consent.
Purpose

How we may use cookies

Cookies and similar technologies may be used for practical website purposes, including functionality, preference storage, basic security, analytics, and website improvement.

  • To remember whether you accepted or declined the cookie banner.
  • To support navigation, layout behavior, and website functionality.
  • To understand basic interaction patterns and improve page clarity.
  • To help maintain website reliability and security.
  • To support analytics or marketing tools where applicable and permitted.
Necessary

Essential technologies

Essential technologies are used to make the website work correctly. They may support core functions such as page display, navigation behavior, mobile menu operation, cookie banner state, and basic security.

These technologies are generally necessary for the website to function and may be used even if optional cookies are declined.

Essential cookies keep the site usable.

They do not exist to build a detailed advertising profile. They support basic website operation and preference handling.

Preferences

Cookie banner and preference storage

This website may use local storage or a similar browser feature to remember your cookie banner choice. For example, if you accept or decline the banner, that choice may be stored in your browser so the banner does not interrupt you on every page.

If you clear your browser storage, use a different browser, or browse from another device, the banner may appear again.

Measurement

Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies or scripts may be used to understand how visitors interact with the website, which pages are visited, and how the user experience can be improved. These tools should be configured according to applicable consent requirements.

  • Analytics may help identify which pages are most useful.
  • Analytics may help detect navigation or performance problems.
  • Analytics should not be used to collect unnecessary sensitive information.
  • Analytics settings may depend on the tools implemented on the website.
External tools

Third-party technologies

This website may load third-party resources such as fonts, icons, maps, analytics, hosting tools, or other embedded services. These providers may process technical information according to their own policies.

We do not control the cookie practices of third-party websites or services. If you interact with third-party links or embedded tools, you should review their cookie and privacy information.

Control

How to manage cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies, delete existing cookies, clear local storage, or set preferences for specific websites.

  • Use your browser settings to delete cookies or local storage.
  • Block cookies if you do not want websites to store them.
  • Use private browsing modes to limit stored data on your device.
  • Review browser help pages for device-specific cookie controls.

Blocking some technologies may affect how the website displays or remembers your choices.

Updates

Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if website functionality, cookie practices, third-party tools, or legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Continued use of the website after updates means you accept the revised Cookie Policy.
